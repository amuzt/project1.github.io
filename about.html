ABOUT US

GoAroundUs is a tour organizer based in Semarang, Indonesia. We are focusing our activities on walking tours in the downtown of Semarang



Why Semarang ?

Because there are many historic buildings from the Dutch colonial heritage, and the place still holds beauty and mystery to this day


Why choose GoAroundUs ?


We are not just an ordinary tour organizer but we love Semarang and its cultural heritage and the natural beauty from the bottom of our hearts

we want to share it with all to our clients all over the world.
